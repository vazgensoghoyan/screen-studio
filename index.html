High-quality videos - no edit skills needed

Things you had to do in video software are done automatically by Screen Studio.

  • Smooth cursor movement

    All shakiness of the mouse cursor is removed giving an instant studio-quality feel.

  • Zoom & follow mouse clicks

    Pick parts of the video you want to focus on the mouse actions and Screen Studio will create all the animations.

  • Cinematic motion blur

    Every moving piece of the animation will have motion blur applied, making the video feel more smooth and perceived FPS higher.

  • Wallpaper & window frame

    Background padding adds breadth to your video. When publishing to social media - videos will feel less busy and noisy.

  • Change layout and cursor

    Record iPhone simulator on your Mac and crop it - all animations will be adjusted automatically. Change your cursor and your mobile app demo is ready.

  • Automatic size balancing

    With one click you can switch horizontal/vertical/square output size and all animations will be re-calculated. It is great for publishing on platforms like Instagram, TikTok, etc.

    Record yourself and switch between you, the screen, or both at once - with amazing animations.

How is Screen Studio different from other screen recording apps?

Screen Studio records your screen as two separate layers: one is the mouse cursor only; 2nd is a screen with the mouse cursor hidden.

Thanks to that, it can apply a lot of effects after the video is recorded, such as making mouse movement smooth, changing cursor size and color, etc.

Most screen recording apps record only one video, which is exactly what you have seen during the recording. Because of that, applying an effect that gives the result video studio quality is impossible.

Is Windows version ready?

No, and there are no near-future plans to add Windows support. Currently, I work on the macOS version only.

Is Screen Studio privacy focused?

Yes. Screen Studio never sends data about your recordings to servers. All features, including generating audio captions, happen on your computer.
